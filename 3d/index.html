UPPAbaby's flagship car seat; I engineered both the V2 and the V3, which garnered a 4.7★ rating on Amazon.

A new base made for the Aria and Mesa Max infant car seats, featuring easy to use SmartSecure install, an anti-rebound panel, and a load leg. Currently receiving a 4.9★ rating on Amazon.

Released in 2020, UPPAbaby's first in a new line of infant car seats following an updated brand design language and advanced features to improve ease-of-use and functionality.

Maxi Cosi Adapters

Adapters to enable attachment of a non-UPPAbaby brand car seat to an UPPAbaby stroller. Designed to easily attach and remove while offering a secure, rigid attachment point.
